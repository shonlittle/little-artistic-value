About the Project

Director.exe is a groundbreaking short film that explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and creative storytelling. Created using cutting-edge AI tools for script generation, visual effects, voice synthesis, and editing, this 6-minute short film blends sharp satire with cinematic flair. The project serves as both a commentary on the future of filmmaking and a demonstration of the creative possibilities when human and artificial intelligence collaborate.
